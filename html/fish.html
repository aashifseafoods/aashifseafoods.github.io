Black Pomphret

The black pomfret is a species of carangid native to reefs of the Indian Ocean and the western Pacific Ocean, where it is found at depths from 15 to 105 m , though it is rarely found deeper than 40 m. This species grows to 75 cm in total length and is very important to local commercial fisheries.
