About Us

Aashif Seafoods is a seafood sourcing company that aims to deliver seafood products to customers all over the world with exquisite quality at very affordable prices.

Who are we

Aashif Seafoods is situated in Bhimavaram, Andhra Pradesh, India, a state which is found to be producing the most no.of volumes in the country. Rich in seafood resources and home to a lot of species habituated in water bodies like lakes, ponds, it makes Andhra Pradesh the only destination for most of the seafood products. By considering all these factors it makes Aashif Seafoods well equipped with the necessary toolkit to aptly understand the trends in the market, the economy of the market that helps us in providing our customers with all the important information to make an informed decision.

By being in the industry for nearly 20 years we understand the interests and choices of a customer and thereby carefully cater them the best of the products available in the market. We always believe in building meaningful relations with customers in order to serve them in a very efficient manner.

Network

For every business that aims to develop a deep-rooted relation with its customers, it has to have a well-knit network that keeps them together. In the case of Aashif Seafoods, we boast a network spanning over 8 developed, technically advanced, populous countries. Each of the countries has great potential in buying huge volumes every year. A tropical country like India is the primary source of Seafood and from this is where the rest of our customers can buy seafood. India provides a very appropriate set of environment for breeding various kinds of seafood.
