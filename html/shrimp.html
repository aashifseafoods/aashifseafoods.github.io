Shrimp

Shrimps constitue nearly 59% of the total seafood exports in the world, thus making them the sole influencing product in the seafood market. Our range of products include versatile breeds of shrimp. From vannamei to karikadi, almost all breeds are supplied throughout the world mainly from countries like India, Bangladesh.
